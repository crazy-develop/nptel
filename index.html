Eligibility

B.Tech Honors Degree

To obtain a B.Tech Honors degree from AKTU, a student must acquire 20 additional credits through NPTEL MOOCs.

  • Must clear all regular B.Tech papers without carry-over.
  • Maintain a CGPA of 7.5 or above throughout the course.
  • Complete 20 credits via NPTEL (approx. 5-6 courses).
  • Courses must be relevant to the discipline or interdisciplinary.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for an NPTEL course?

You can register through the Swayam portal (swayam.gov.in). Create an account, search for the course, and click 'Join'.

Is the exam fee refundable?

No, the exam fee is generally non-refundable. However, in specific cases like medical emergencies, NPTEL may consider requests.

How are credits transferred to AKTU marksheet?

After passing the exam, you need to submit your certificate to your college registrar. The university will then update your marksheet.
